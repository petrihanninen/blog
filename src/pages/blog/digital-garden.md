It's decided, I'm not going to take this blog seriously. Also, I'm not going to write for anybody else. Instead, I'll be writing these short and sweet "seeds" for me, myself and I.

I'm not a writer, and I absolutely hate the writers' block, which apparently hits like Mike Tyson after two bad and one useless post. I find my struggling with that ridiculous, as those two bad posts were part of a three-part series.

Intsead, I'll try cultivating the idea of a digital garden: a place, where small ideas are thrown in and left to grow. Some of those ideas might die, some might bloom, some might be still growing when you read them.
