## And we're live!

After a couple of mishaps with quotes and the  structure of the template, I actually managed to publish this post. I got things rolling quite smoothly with an app called <a href="https://apps.apple.com/fi/app/working-copy-git-client/id896694807">Working Copy</a> - aptly named as it seems to work.

The biggest annoyance I have is that I can't use designer quotes or anything, but I think that's fine. Also, there could be a few improvements to the UI of the app (some bits of text get hidden behind some icons and stuff). Oh, and double-tapping spacebar for a period and a space doesn't work, which is quite painstaking as well.

That being said, the experience is quite nice and worth it, if github is the only cms you have, or if you need to fix this, that and the other thing on the go.

For pushing your commits you need the pro version of the app. You can get a 10 day free trial, which I used for this test, but aafter that a one-time fee of around 20 € is required  I might actually buy it, we'll see.
